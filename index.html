About Us

Tezza Business Solutions Ltd is a “niche” service provider of Software Development, Quality Assurance and Software Testing services. Beginning its operations in 2000 as a Web Development company in the US, we’ve since evolved into a Services-oriented company who only engages in Product development as a value-add service to our customers who continue to appreciate our “Customer First” Service Delivery approach.

Our Internship Program

Our Internship program is an “unpaid” Internship Program designed to expose University students in their final year of school to aspects of our business. At Tezza Business Solutions, our Internship Program exposes final year University Students to REAL projects giving them an opportunity to apply their skills and capabilities to new and ongoing projects. Our program extends beyond our core competencies to all areas of our organization such as HR, Logistics, and Sales & Marketing. Looking to have fun while helping our clients reduce cost, improve on overall quality while reducing their time to market? Are you a self-starter with a “can do” attitude? Is your course of study one of the following – Marketing, Business Management, Information Systems Management, Computer Science, Mass Communication, Journalism, English Language, English Literature, Law? If so, come join our program and get a head start on your career. Benefits – Consideration for hire post graduation – Hands-on, on-the-job training
